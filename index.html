WHATSAPP MESSENGER 

STEPS FOR DOWNLOADING AND  INSTALLING WHATSAPP MESSENGER APPLICATION FOR IOS :-

  1. Go to the “App Store” in the phone.

  2. Click on the “Search” option and search for WhatsApp Messenger.

  3. Once the application is searched, click on the “Get” button to download the application.

  4. It will ask for authentication and based your authentication preferences, input the password or complete the face recognition to continue the downloading.

  5. Once the download is complete, it will be installed on the phone.

  6. Now, open the application. 

  7. Click on “Agree and continue”  button to accept the Privacy policies and the Terms of Service(you can read these by clicking on the hyperlinks).

  8. The next step is to verify your cell phone number, select the correct country and enter your phone number.

  9. Click on “Next”

  10. Please check if the number entered is correct and then click on “ok”. 

  11. On clicking the “ok” button, you will receive an SMS to the entered number which will be a 6 digit code.

  12. If you do not happen to receive the code in a minute, you can choose the option to “resend SMS” or “Call me”(you will receive a call on the entered number and a code will be given).

  13. Once you get the code - enter it and you will be directed to the next step.

  14. The application will now give you an option to find and restore the backup, if you wish to restore the backup, press on “continue” and if you do not wish to, press on “not now” option.

  15. If you select the option “Not now”, you will be directed to a page where you will have to add your name and profile picture.

  16. The profile picture can be selected from the phone’s gallery by allowing the application access to it or you can click a new image and set it as a profile picture(you will have to allow the application to access your phone camera).

  17. Now, your whatsapp messenger is almost ready to use, to start chatting with friends and family - click on the “Settings” options and allow the application to access your contacts so that it will show your contacts who are available on this messenger.

  18. Now you can click on “Chat” button and select the name of the person to chat with them.
