<h1> WHATSAPP MESSENGER</h1>

<p> The WhatsApp messenger is a simple messaging application where in one can chat, make voice and video calls  with their friends and family instantly. It is a free of cost application available for all the platforms like Android, IOS and Windows mobile phones and can also be used on Windows and Mac desktop computers.WhatsApp messenger helps us in staying connected with our loved ones and is a very easy to use application.</p>


<h1>IMPORTANT FEATURES OF WHATSAPP</h1>

<ul>
  <li>This application is absolutely free and just requires internet connectivity to function.</li>
  <li>This application can be used for chatting, making voice and video calls and also for transferring money using the UPI.</li>
  <li>We can make group voice and video calls(maximum limit is 4 people). </li>

We can create a group of 256 people and text them all at once.

  <li>This application can also be used for transferring money to friends and family directly into their bank account.</li>

  <li>This application can be used on computer as well using the “WhatsApp Web/Desktop” option.</li>

      <li>We can share our location and “live” location using this application. The “live location” feature is that the location is updated as you move ahead and the person with whom the location is shared with gets the current location.</li>

      <li>We can share images, videos, audio files, contacts and documents as well.</li>

      <li>We can star mark important messages and access them quickly by using the “Starred messages” in the “settings” menu.</li>

      <li>We can share emoticons and also GIFs to express ourselves better.</li>

      <li>We can keep status which disappears in 24 hours and this status can be an image, video or simple text and we can also check who all have seen our status. We can also keep this status for a closed group of people only.</li>
    </ul>


<h1>  STEPS FOR DOWNLOADING AND  INSTALLING WHATSAPP MESSENGER APPLICATION FOR IOS :- </h1>

<ol type="1">
<li> Go to the “App Store” in the phone.</li>

    <li>Click on the “Search” option and search for WhatsApp Messenger.</li>

    <li>Once the application is searched, click on the “Get” button to download the application.</li>

    <li>It will ask for authentication and based your authentication preferences, input the password or complete the face recognition to continue the downloading.</li>

    <li>Once the download is complete, it will be installed on the phone.</li>

    <li>Now, open the application. </li>

    <li>Click on “Agree and continue”  button to accept the Privacy policies and the Terms of Service(you can read these by clicking on the hyperlinks).</li>

    <li>The next step is to verify your cell phone number, select the correct country and enter your phone number.</li>

    <li>Click on “Next”</li>

    <li>Please check if the number entered is correct and then click on “ok”. </li>

    <li>On clicking the “ok” button, you will receive an SMS to the entered number which will be a 6 digit code.</li>

    <li>If you do not happen to receive the code in a minute, you can choose the option to “resend SMS” or “Call me”(you will receive a call on the entered number and a code will be given).</li>

    <li>Once you get the code - enter it and you will be directed to the next step.</li>

    <li>The application will now give you an option to find and restore the backup, if you wish to restore the backup, press on “continue” and if you do not wish to, press on “not now” option.</li>

    <li>If you select the option “Not now”, you will be directed to a page where you will have to add your name and profile picture.</li>

    <li>The profile picture can be selected from the phone’s gallery by allowing the application access to it or you can click a new image and set it as a profile picture(you will have to allow the application to access your phone camera).</li>

    <li>Now, your whatsapp messenger is almost ready to use, to start chatting with friends and family - click on the “Settings” options and allow the application to access your contacts so that it will show your contacts who are available on this messenger.</li>

    <li>Now you can click on “Chat” button and select the name of the person to chat with them.</li>


</ol>
